About Me

Gavin Darling is a multimedia artist and designer. Art is a language that transcends words, so he finds joy communicating in all forms of art from web design and photography to music and fine art. He is currently studying multimedia production at Point Park University in Pittsburgh, PA with a minor in graphic design to develop skills in graphic design, photography, video, and website design.

In his spare time, Gavin immerses himself in his art and music to illuminate the beauty of nature through pen, marker, and paint as well as photography and music.
